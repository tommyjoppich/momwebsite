Biography

Mom was born on March 7, 1973, twenty-nine years ago. As the thirteenth of fourteen children, Mom had no shrotage of friends at home. Growing up in Warren, MI, was an adventure every day, but Mom wouldn't have had it any other way. After finishing school, Mom began work at a law firm as an administrative assistant. This is how she met Dad. Shortly after marriage, Mom found out that she was pregnant with her first son who would later come to be known as Joey. Mom was now officially a Mom. Two years later, a second son came along: Tommy. With her family now complete, Mom settled into life in their Lake Orion, MI, home. After raising both of her boys for 20 years, Mom welcomed a new child into the home, a mini golden doodle named Chloe. As Joey and Tommy move off to school, Chloe now keeps Mom company at their home in Lake Orion as Mom adjusts to the empty nest lifestyle yet continues to be a strong and loving mother.
